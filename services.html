Prenatal Massage

NURTURING | SOOTHING | REBALANCING

Prenatal massage is designed to support the changing needs of the body during pregnancy. Using gentle, soothing techniques, it helps reduce muscle tension, ease back pain, and improve overall comfort. The massage is performed in side-lying or specially supported positions to ensure safety and relaxation. Ideal for expectant mothers seeking relief from stress and physical discomfort. This treatment is available for clients in their second or third trimester (after 12 weeks).
